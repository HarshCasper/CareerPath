The Uplift Project

Uplift Project is a global-remote initiative for people worldwide who want to talk about something fruitful. This Project is a part of HTML-CSS-JS Team 04.

What is it all about?

The primary purpose for our Project is to create a Website for Students to get the required Career Paths in various fields which they can follow to excel in their future. With seperate sections, where they can view recent Internship Opportunities, Learning Courses that they can undertake and Blogs which will allow them to better understand the need of the future and more, the website tries to serve your purpose.
